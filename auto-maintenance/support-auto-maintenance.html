Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Open the app and tap "Sign Up" on the welcome screen. You can register with your email or use Google/Apple sign-in for quick access.

How do I sync my data across devices?

Create an account and sign in on all your devices. Your data will automatically sync via the cloud when you're connected to the internet.

Can I export my data?

Yes! Go to Settings ‚Üí Data Management ‚Üí Export Data. You can export your vehicle records in CSV or PDF format.

How do I cancel my subscription?

For iOS: Settings ‚Üí Your Name ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí Auto Maintenance ‚Üí Cancel
For Android: Google Play Store ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí Auto Maintenance ‚Üí Cancel

I lost my data. Can I recover it?

If you have an account with cloud sync enabled, your data is automatically backed up. Simply sign in again to restore your data. Guest users should enable cloud sync to prevent data loss.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Account Settings ‚Üí Delete Account. Please note this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data both in transit and at rest. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.
